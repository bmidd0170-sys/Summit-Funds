# Daily Spending Schedule - Quick Reference

## What's New

The AI Budget Advisor now has a **Daily Schedule** view that shows users exactly what they should spend each hour of the day, based on their financial profile and economic conditions.

## Key Features

### 📊 Monthly Overview (Original)

- Total income and expenses
- 50/30/20 budget recommendation
- Spending comparison charts
- AI recommendations

### 📅 Daily Schedule (NEW)

- Hour-by-hour spending breakdown
- Specific activities for each time period
- Smart money-saving tips
- Budget allocation visualization
- Economic insights and recommendations

## How to Use

### 1. Set Up Your Profile

Navigate to **Financial Profile** and enter:

- Monthly income
- Essential expenses (housing, utilities, groceries, transport, insurance, phone, internet)
- Debt payments (credit card, student loans, personal loans)
- Discretionary spending (dining, entertainment, subscriptions, shopping, gym)

### 2. View Daily Schedule

1. Go to **AI Budget Advisor**
2. Click **📅 Daily Schedule** button
3. View today's recommended spending plan

### 3. Navigate Through Days

- **← Previous**: View yesterday's schedule
- **Next →**: View tomorrow's schedule
- **Today**: Return to current date

### 4. Understand the Schedule

Each day shows:

#### Daily Overview

- Your daily budget (derived from monthly income ÷ 30)
- Potential savings amount

#### Hourly Schedule (7 time slots)

Example:

```
6:00 AM - 9:00 AM (Morning)
💵 $8.50
🍳 Breakfast and morning commute
💡 Tips:
  ✓ Have breakfast at home
  ✓ Use public transit if possible
```

#### Budget Allocation (50/30/20)

- **🏠 Essentials (50%)**: $X.XX for needs
- **🎉 Discretionary (30%)**: $X.XX for wants
- **💰 Savings Goal (20%)**: $X.XX to save

#### Economic Insights

- Current financial recommendations
- Daily challenge to track spending

| Time       | Period          | Purpose                |

| 9-12 PM    | Mid-Morning     | Work/study time        |

| 1-5 PM     | Afternoon       | Work/activities        |
| 5-7 PM     | Evening Commute | Travel home            |
| 7-10 PM    | Evening         | Dining & entertainment |
| 10 PM-6 AM | Night           | Sleep                  |

## Smart Money Tips

The schedule includes specific tips like:

- "Have breakfast at home" for Morning period
- "Use public transit if possible" for commute times
- "Bring coffee from home" to save on mid-morning snacks
- "Avoid mid-morning snacks" during work hours
- "Limit dining out to budgeted amount" for evening
- "Choose free entertainment" options

## Budget Breakdown Examples

### 50/30/20 Rule

- **50% Essentials**: Housing, food, transport, utilities, insurance
- **30% Discretionary**: Entertainment, dining out, shopping, subscriptions
- **20% Savings**: Emergency fund, investments, future goals

### Daily Example

If your daily budget is $100:

- Essentials: $50 (morning commute, lunch, groceries)
- Discretionary: $30 (coffee, snacks, entertainment)
- Savings: $20 (automated transfer to savings)

## AI-Powered Features

The daily schedule is generated using:

- **Your financial profile**: Income, expenses, spending habits
- **Economic analysis**: AI considers current economy factors
- **Time-based optimization**: Different spending patterns throughout the day
- **Smart recommendations**: Contextual tips for each period

If OpenAI API is unavailable, the app automatically uses local intelligent generation.

## Troubleshooting

**Schedule not loading?**

- Ensure financial profile is complete
- Monthly income must be set
- Check browser console for errors

**Recommendations seem off?**

- Update your financial profile with accurate data
- Ensure income and expenses reflect your actual situation
- The AI learns from your actual spending patterns

**Want different time slots?**

- Current slots are optimized based on typical daily patterns
- Future updates may allow customization
